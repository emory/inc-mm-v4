on my dresser <i>(glass <br />
of water)</i> i found an<br />
elastic band <i>(nearly <br />
in-<br />
visible)</i><br />
that mere days ago <br />
held<br />
your hair <br />
and <br />
i stared at it<br />
in my palm,<br />
to imagine,<br />
and re-<br />
member,<br />
your hair<br />
in my face <br />
<i>when <br />
your delirium <br />
set<br />
in.</i><br />
