Her nail polish<br />
is called<br />

> "Excuse Me, <br />
> Sir",<br />

**but**<br /> 
she never<br />
<br />
*(ever)*<br />
has anything but<br />
my full<br />
<br />
and<br/>
***utterly rapt***<br />
<br />
***attention.***<br />
